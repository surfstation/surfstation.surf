      About
      Here in North East FL, there are lots of ways to be active and enjoy the outdoors. But, the weather changes fast and plays a huge role in determining which activities will make the best use of your day. This site is our collection of the best local weather resources to help you figure that out. Whether you want to:
      • surf the waves
      • SUP along the intracoastal
      • kayak through Salt Run
      • walk downtown
      • run or bike the miles of white sand beaches
      • get a more aquatic view of the Oldest City jet skiing, boating, or sailing
      • fish off a pier
      • kitesurf down a windy coast
      • or chillax on the beach with a cold drink and the stray rays
      Check here first to get live weather data from our weather station and cams located in the heart of St. Augustine Beach.
